About PoSH

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH) Act, 2013 is a landmark Indian law that mandates every employer to create a safe, respectful, and harassment-free workplace for women.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Who needs PoSH training?

All employees, managers, and Internal Committee (IC) members in organizations with 10+ employees.

Q. What happens if a company doesn’t comply?

Non-compliance can lead to penalties up to ₹50,000, reputational damage, and in severe cases, cancellation of business licenses.

Q. Do men also need PoSH training?

Yes. The law mandates all employees — men, women, interns, and contractors — must be trained.

Q. Will completing the course make our organization compliant?

Training + Internal Committee setup + annual reporting = full compliance. We help you at every step.

Q. Can you customize training for our industry?

Absolutely. We adapt content with sector-specific case studies so it resonates with your teams.
